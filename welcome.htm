What does the word "Horoscope" mean?

 

Horoscope comes from the greek word "oroskòpos" (he who looks at the time or gives the time).
It is made of two words "òra" (time, season) and "skòpeo" (looking, observing).
Horoscope is the zodiac sign that rose at the horizon at the time of birth.
With the word Horoscope it is meant the natal chart. At the time of birth the stars were in a certain
 position in a certain zodiac sign, making either armonius or discordant. The Risign Sign determines
 the First House, and every house has a particular meaning.
